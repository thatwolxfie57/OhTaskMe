About OhTaskMe

A comprehensive task and event management application

Our Mission

OhTaskMe was created with a simple mission: to help people organize their lives, reduce stress, and never miss important deadlines or events. We believe that with the right tools, anyone can become more productive and achieve their goals with less effort.

Our application combines task management with event planning, creating a unified system that helps you prepare for important events and manage your day-to-day responsibilities.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive task management
  • Event planning with automatic task generation
  • AI-powered task suggestions
  • Customizable dashboard and statistics
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile
